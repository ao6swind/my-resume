Digital exam system could be divided into examiner-end (socket server) and examinee-end(socket client). The examinee-end system could decrypt exam package(export from paper factory system) and display question in diffenet type. Before examinee admission, examiner should setting exam system. After all examinee have a seat, examiner will control exam progress. Examinee could do device test(headphone or microphone). If these is no any problem, examinee-end will display questions after exam ringtone sounded. After exam, examinee-end will encrypt all information and upload to examiner-end.
